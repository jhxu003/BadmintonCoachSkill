## Platform Boundary

- YouTube is excluded by project decision and not part of completion accounting.
- Douyin public profile access still lacks a stable per-video metadata export.
- The indexed Instagram reel still cannot be fetched reliably.
- These rows remain discovery or access-gap records and cannot support deterministic technical rules.

Newly discovered accessible Bilibili sources are not automatically covered. They must pass the same deduplication, full-audio ASR, timestamp review, visual/Pose processing where applicable, evidence reduction, and completion audit before the corpus can again be called complete against its index.

## Promotion Boundary

- ASR evidence may route topics, questions, frameworks, and timestamp lookup.
- ASR-only evidence may support conceptual, equipment, or player-fit routing but never visible mechanics.
- Sparse VLM stills may support visibility descriptions but not motion or force claims.
- Dense monocular Pose may support coarse 2D change descriptions but not racket-face, shuttle-contact, grip-pressure, calibrated 3D, force, or true internal rotation claims.
- No model-derived evidence is described as Liu Hui's exact wording, a human coach review, official authorization, or endorsement.
