# Evidence Policy

Use three public-source tiers:

- Tier A: Zheng Siwei's verified official public account. It may support teaching direction after title or content review, but it is not an endorsement of this Skill.
- Tier B: traceable public instruction or institutional course material. Promote a claim only after instructor identity, access boundary, and the relevant content window are reviewed.
- Tier C: third-party analysis, observation footage, and match replay. Use it for questions, counterexamples, and bounded visible-pattern candidates only.

A deterministic rule requires direct structured learner evidence plus Tier A support, or a reviewed Tier A/B chain. Tier C alone must never create, strengthen, or settle a rule.

Keep public files to source metadata and public-safe summaries. Do not publish downloaded media, screenshots, ASR, model traces, cache keys, internal paths, credentials, or copyrighted course reconstructions.

For four-player video, require explicit learner and partner selection plus four-corner court confirmation. Report TrackNet-style shuttle output as temporal heatmap candidates with confidence, alternatives, and occlusion. Never report an exact shuttle-racket contact, tactical intent, 3D location, force, or biomechanics unless independently measured.
