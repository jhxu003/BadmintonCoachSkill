# Corpus Provenance

This Skill is a non-official synthesis of Li Yuxuan public-source material indexed in `data/coaches/li-yuxuan/source-index.tsv`.

- Scope: 382 accessible public official-account Bilibili videos, plus public account and paid-course boundary records used only for source accounting.
- Extraction: private ASR, semantic candidate extraction, sparse visual visibility review, and selected dense 2D pose are reduced into public-safe metadata. Raw media, ASR transcripts, screenshots, prompts, model output, and private paths are not published.
- Promotion rule: semantic candidates require cross-source consistency review. Action claims additionally require compatible visual evidence where the claim depends on a visible action.
- Copyright boundary: paid course pages establish topic boundaries only. The Skill does not copy, transcribe, reconstruct, or distribute paid lesson content.
- Evidence boundary: source IDs support the synthesis and routing. They are not a claim of personal review, authorization, or endorsement by Li Yuxuan.
