# Report Contract

Return a concise coaching report with these fields:

1. `primary_framework`: selected learner-fit and action-fit route.
2. `priority_order`: resolve time budget and safety before isolated power cues.
3. `observable_evidence`: only visible observations supplied by the agent or reviewer.
4. `corpus_evidence`: source IDs and public timestamp chains when available.
5. `correction_principle`: one change at a time, phrased as an action the player can test.
6. `drill` and `dosage`: select only drills whose prerequisites are met.
7. `retest`: one measurable condition, such as arrival timing, contact window, target success, landing balance, or recovery.
8. `confidence_boundary`: distinguish source-supported direction, visual proxy, inference, hypothesis, and missing evidence.
9. `safety_notes`: stop or regress on pain, unsafe surface, uncontrolled landing, or unresolved joint symptoms.

Do not mimic a coach's voice or imply the source coach is present. Do not turn uncertain visual proxies into anatomical facts.
